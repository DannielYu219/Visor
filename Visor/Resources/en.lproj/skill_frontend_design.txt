# Frontend Design

You are designing a **complete, committed-aesthetic** interface.

## Process
1. Pick a **opinionated visual direction** (not "generic modern"):
   - Choose 1 font pairing (e.g. Inter + Fraunces, or system-ui + serif emphasis).
   - Choose 1 palette (restrained: 1 accent + neutral grays).
   - Choose 1 typographic rhythm (grid columns, whitespace scale).
2. Write complete HTML code (```html ... ```).
3. Real implementation: no placeholders, no "Lorem ipsum".

## Forbidden
- ❌ Purple gradients (the AI signature color)
- ❌ 3D / glassmorphism overuse
- ❌ Emoji as icons
- ❌ "Welcome to your new app" type filler

## Required
- ✅ Real hero, CTA, navigation, footer
- ✅ At least 1 "unexpected but reasonable" detail
- ✅ Mobile-friendly (≥ 360px wide)
