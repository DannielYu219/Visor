# Taste Skill: Anti-Slop Frontend Design Rules

> Adapted from taste-skill by Leonxlnx (MIT License) for Visor's single-HTML-artifact context.
> Every rule below is contextual. First read the brief, then pull only what fits.

---

## 0. BRIEF INFERENCE

Before writing code, infer what the user actually wants. Read these signals:
1. Page kind - landing / portfolio / redesign / editorial / slide / wireframe
2. Vibe words - "minimalist", "calm", "premium", "brutalist", "playful", "editorial"
3. Audience - B2B / consumer / recruiter / creative
4. Quiet constraints - accessibility-first, regulated, trust-first

Output a one-line "Design Read" before generating:
"Reading this as: <page kind> for <audience>, with a <vibe> language, leaning toward <aesthetic family>."

If the brief is ambiguous, ask ONE clarifying question. If you can confidently infer, declare the read and proceed.

### Anti-Default Discipline
Do NOT default to: AI-purple gradients, centered hero over dark mesh, three equal feature cards, generic glassmorphism everywhere, Inter + slate-900. These are LLM defaults. Reach past them.

---

## 1. THREE DIALS

Set three dials after the design read. Every layout, motion, and density decision is gated by these.

- DESIGN_VARIANCE: 8 (1 = perfect symmetry, 10 = artsy chaos)
- MOTION_INTENSITY: 6 (1 = static, 10 = cinematic physics)
- VISUAL_DENSITY: 4 (1 = airy gallery, 10 = packed cockpit)

Baseline: 8 / 6 / 4. Override conversationally based on the brief.

### Dial Inference
| Signal | VARIANCE | MOTION | DENSITY |
|---|---|---|---|
| minimalist / clean / calm / editorial | 5-6 | 3-4 | 2-3 |
| premium consumer / Apple-y / luxury | 7-8 | 5-7 | 3-4 |
| playful / wild / Awwwards / experimental | 9-10 | 8-10 | 3-4 |
| landing / portfolio / marketing (default) | 7-9 | 6-8 | 3-5 |
| trust-first / public-sector / regulated | 3-4 | 2-3 | 4-5 |

---

## 2. TYPOGRAPHY

- Display headlines: large scale, tight tracking, controlled hierarchy via weight + color, not just raw scale.
- Body: readable line-height, max-width ~65ch for paragraphs.
- Sans font choice:
  - DISCOURAGED as default: Inter. Pick Geist, Outfit, Cabinet Grotesk, Satoshi, or a brand-appropriate serif first.
  - Override: Inter acceptable when user explicitly asks for neutral / Linear-style feel.
- SERIF DISCIPLINE (very discouraged as default):
  - Serif is only acceptable when the brand brief literally names a serif, OR the aesthetic is genuinely editorial / luxury / publication.
  - Specifically BANNED as defaults: Fraunces, Instrument_Serif.
  - For everything else (agency, studio, modern brand, premium consumer, portfolio), default sans-serif display.
- EMPHASIS RULE: use italic or bold of the SAME font. Do NOT inject a random serif word into a sans headline.
- ITALIC DESCENDER CLEARANCE: when italic display type has descender letters (y g j p q), use leading 1.1+ minimum. Audit every italic word.

---

## 3. COLOR

- Max 1 accent color. Saturation under 80% by default.
- THE LILA RULE: AI purple/blue glow aesthetic is discouraged. No automatic purple button glows, no random neon gradients. Use neutral bases (zinc/slate/stone) with high-contrast singular accents (emerald, electric blue, deep rose, burnt orange).
- Override: if brand explicitly asks for purple, embrace it with intent: consistent palette, harmonised neutrals, restrained gradients.
- One palette per project. Do not fluctuate between warm and cool grays.
- COLOR CONSISTENCY LOCK: once an accent is chosen, use it on the WHOLE page. A warm-grey site does not get a blue CTA in section 7.
- PREMIUM-CONSUMER PALETTE BAN: for premium-consumer briefs (cookware, wellness, artisan, luxury), the default beige+brass+oxblood+espresso palette is BANNED. Rotate alternatives: cold luxury (silver+chrome), forest (green+bone+amber), black and tan, cobalt+cream, terracotta+slate, pure monochrome + one saturated pop.
- No pure #000000 or pure #ffffff. Use off-black (zinc-950) and off-white.

---

## 4. LAYOUT DISCIPLINE (Hard Rules)

- Hero MUST fit in the initial viewport. Headline max 2 lines, subtext max 20 words and 3-4 lines, CTAs visible without scroll.
- HERO TOP PADDING CAP: max ~6rem at desktop. More than that reads as a layout bug.
- HERO STACK DISCIPLINE: max 4 text elements (eyebrow OR brand strip, headline, subtext, CTAs). No tiny tagline below CTAs, no trust micro-strip in hero, no feature bullet list in hero.
- "Used by" logo wall belongs UNDER the hero, never inside it.
- ANTI-CENTER BIAS: centered hero avoided when DESIGN_VARIANCE > 4. Force split-screen, left-aligned content, or asymmetric white-space.
- NO 3-column equal feature cards. The generic "three identical cards horizontally" is banned. Use 2-column zig-zag, asymmetric grid, or horizontal-scroll.
- Navigation MUST render on a single line at desktop. Height max 80px.
- Bento grids MUST have rhythm. Vary composition: alternate full-width rows, asymmetric tile sizes, vertical breaks. N items = N cells, no empty cells.
- Section-Layout-Repetition Ban: once you use a layout family for a section, it can appear at most ONCE on the page. A page with 8 sections must use at least 4 different layout families.
- ZIGZAG ALTERNATION CAP: max 2 consecutive sections with left-image/right-text pattern. The 3rd is a Pre-Flight Fail.
- EYEBROW RESTRAINT: maximum 1 eyebrow per 3 sections. Hero counts as 1. Instead of an eyebrow, drop it entirely - the headline alone is enough.
- SPLIT-HEADER BAN: "left big headline + right small explainer paragraph" as a section header is banned. Stack vertically.
- Bento Background Diversity: at least 2-3 cells need real visual variation (image, gradient, pattern), not all white-on-white text cards.
- SHAPE CONSISTENCY LOCK: pick ONE corner-radius scale for the page. All-sharp, all-soft (12-16px), or all-pill. Mixed without a documented rule is broken design.
- Mobile collapse must be explicit for every multi-column layout. Declare the <768px fallback.

---

## 5. INTERACTIVE UI STATES

Always implement full cycles:
- Loading: skeletal loaders matching final layout shape. Avoid generic spinners.
- Empty States: beautifully composed; indicate how to populate.
- Error States: clear, inline for forms, toasts for transient.
- Tactile Feedback: on :active, use slight translate or scale to simulate push.
- BUTTON CONTRAST CHECK: verify button text is readable against background. WCAG AA min 4.5:1. No white-on-white.
- CTA BUTTON WRAP BAN: button text MUST fit on one line at desktop. Shorten label (3 words max for primary CTAs) or widen button.
- NO DUPLICATE CTA INTENT: two CTAs with same intent on one page is a Fail. "Get in touch" + "Contact us" + "Let's talk" = pick ONE.
- FORM CONTRAST CHECK: inputs, placeholders, focus rings, labels all pass WCAG AA.

---

## 6. CONTENT DENSITY

- Default per section: short headline (max 8 words) + short sub-paragraph (max 25 words) + one visual OR one CTA.
- No data-dump sections. Top 3-5 highlights + "View full list" link.
- Long lists need a different UI component, not a longer list. Use card grid, tabs, accordion, carousel, or marquee for >5 items.
- COPY SELF-AUDIT: re-read every visible string before shipping. Flag grammatically broken, unclear referents, AI-hallucination phrases, mock-poetic micro-meta. Rewrite every flagged string.
- Fake-precise numbers are banned unless from real data or explicitly labeled as mock.
- One copy register per page. Do not mix technical mono, editorial prose, and marketing punch.

---

## 7. QUOTES AND TESTIMONIALS

- Max 3 lines of quote body. If longer, cut it.
- Attribution: name + role + optionally company. Never name only.
- Use real typographic quotes or none. Not straight ASCII.
- No em-dashes inside quote text.

---

## 8. PAGE THEME LOCK

- The page has ONE theme. Sections do not invert.
- If dark mode, ALL sections are dark. No light-mode section sandwiched between dark sections.
- Default: pick light, dark, or auto at the page level and lock it.
- Respect prefers-color-scheme. Design for both modes from the start.

---

## 9. IMAGE AND VISUAL ASSET STRATEGY

Landing pages are visual products. Text-only pages with fake-screenshot divs are slop.

Priority order:
1. Image-generation tool first. Generate section-specific assets at the right aspect ratio.
2. Real web images: use https://picsum.photos/seed/{descriptive-seed}/{w}/{h} for placeholder photography.
3. Last resort: leave clearly-labeled placeholder slots and tell the user.

Real company logos for social proof: use Simple Icons CDN (https://cdn.simpleicons.org/{slug}/ffffff) or generate SVG monogram marks. Do NOT use plain text wordmarks. Logo wall = logos only, no industry/category labels below.

Hand-rolled decorative SVGs strongly discouraged. Div-based fake screenshots (fake task lists, fake dashboards) are BANNED. Hero needs a real visual - text + gradient blob is not a hero.

---

## 10. MOTION AND INTERACTION

- Motion must be motivated. Before adding animation, ask "what does this communicate?" Valid: hierarchy, storytelling, feedback, state transition. Invalid: "it looked cool".
- MARQUEE MAX-ONE-PER-PAGE: horizontal scrolling text marquees at most once per page.
- Animate ONLY transform and opacity. Never animate top, left, width, height.
- Use will-change sparingly.
- Reduced Motion (mandatory): any motion above MOTION_INTENSITY 3 MUST honor prefers-reduced-motion. Gate animations behind @media (prefers-reduced-motion: no-preference) or provide override.
- No window.addEventListener('scroll'). Use IntersectionObserver, CSS scroll-driven animations, or requestAnimationFrame with care.
- Perpetual micro-interactions (pulse, typewriter, float, shimmer): use sparingly, not on every card. Apply spring physics, no linear easing.
- Motion claimed = motion shown. If MOTION_INTENSITY > 4, the page must actually move. If you cannot ship working motion, drop the dial to 3.

## 11. AI TELLS (Forbidden Patterns)

### Visual and CSS
- NO neon/outer glows by default. Use inner borders or subtle tinted shadows.
- NO pure black (#000000). Use off-black.
- NO oversaturated accents. Desaturate to blend with neutrals.
- NO excessive gradient text for large headers.
- NO custom mouse cursors.

### Typography
- AVOID Inter as default.
- NO oversized H1s that just scream. Control hierarchy with weight + color.
- NO em-dash anywhere. Em-dash is COMPLETELY banned in headlines, eyebrows, pills, body, quotes, attribution, captions, buttons, alt text. Use period, comma, parentheses, colon, or regular hyphen. This is non-negotiable.

### Layout and Spacing
- NO 3-column equal feature cards.
- NO version labels in hero (V0.6, BETA, INVITE-ONLY) unless brief is a launch.
- NO section-number eyebrows (00 / INDEX, 001 Capabilities).
- NO decorative colored status dots on every list/nav/badge.
- NO em-dash as design element.
- NO br-broken-and-italicized headlines as default "design move".
- NO vertical rotated text (agency-portfolio cliche).
- NO crosshair/hairline grid lines as decoration.

### Fake Product Previews
- NO div-based fake product UI in hero (fake task list, fake terminal, fake dashboard).
- NO fake version footers inside fake screenshots.

### Marketing Copy Tells
- NO "Quietly in use at" / "Quietly trusted by" headers. Use "Trusted by" or skip.
- NO "From the field" / "Field notes" / "Currently on the bench" poetic labels.
- NO weather/locale strips ("LIS 14:23 18C") unless brief is place-focused.
- NO micro-meta-sentences under eyebrows.
- NO generic step labels ("Stage 1 / Stage 2"). Use verb-noun directly.
- NO pills/labels overlaid on images.
- NO photo-credit captions as decoration.
- NO version footers on marketing pages.
- NO decoration text strip at hero bottom ("BRAND. MOTION. SPATIAL.").
- NO floating top-right sub-text in section headings.
- NO scoring/progress bars with filled background tracks.
- NO scroll cues ("Scroll", "scroll to explore").
- NO border-t + border-b on every row of long lists.
- NO "Jane Doe" generic names. Use creative, realistic, locale-appropriate names.
- NO fake-perfect numbers (99.99%, 50%). Use organic, messy data.
- NO startup-slop brand names (Acme, Nexus, SmartFlow, Cloudly).
- NO filler verbs (Elevate, Seamless, Unleash, Next-Gen, Revolutionize).

### Em-Dash Ban
Em-dash is COMPLETELY banned. Zero em-dashes on the page. If output contains a single em-dash anywhere visible, the output fails. Use regular hyphen for ranges, periods for sentence breaks, commas for clauses.

## 12. PRE-FLIGHT CHECK

Run this matrix before outputting code. If any box fails, the output is not done.

- Brief inference declared (one-liner)?
- Dial values explicit and reasoned from brief?
- ZERO em-dashes anywhere on the page?
- Page Theme Lock: ONE theme for the whole page?
- Color Consistency Lock: one accent color across all sections?
- Shape Consistency Lock: one corner-radius system?
- Button Contrast Check: every CTA readable against background?
- CTA Button Wrap: no label wraps to 2+ lines at desktop?
- No Duplicate CTA Intent: no two CTAs with same intent?
- Serif discipline: if serif used, NOT Fraunces or Instrument_Serif?
- Italic descender clearance: every italic word with y g j p q has leading 1.1+?
- Hero fits viewport: headline max 2 lines, subtext max 20 words, CTA visible without scroll?
- Hero top padding: max ~6rem at desktop?
- Hero stack discipline: max 4 text elements?
- Eyebrow count: max 1 per 3 sections?
- No Split-Header pattern?
- Zigzag alternation: no 3+ consecutive same pattern?
- Logo wall under hero, not inside it, uses real SVG logos?
- Bento has rhythm AND exact cell count (no empty cells)?
- Bento background diversity: at least 2-3 cells have visual variation?
- Copy Self-Audit: no broken or AI-hallucinated phrases?
- Motion motivated: every animation justified in one sentence?
- Marquee max-one-per-page?
- Navigation on ONE line at desktop, height max 80px?
- Section-Layout-Repetition: no two sections share same layout family?
- Long lists use the right UI component (not default ul with divide-y for >5 items)?
- Real images used (gen-tool, then picsum-seed, then placeholder slots)?
- No div-based fake screenshots?
- No pills/labels overlaid on images?
- No photo-credit captions as decoration?
- No version footers on marketing pages?
- No micro-meta-sentences under eyebrows?
- No decoration text strip at hero bottom?
- No locale/time/weather strips unless brief is place-focused?
- No scroll cues?
- No version labels in hero?
- No section-numbering eyebrows?
- No decorative dots?
- No border-t + border-b on every row of long lists?
- Content density sane: no 20-row data tables, max 25-word sub-paragraphs?
- Quotes max 3 lines, attribution clean (no em-dash)?
- Motion claimed = motion shown?
- Reduced motion honored for MOTION_INTENSITY > 3?
- Dark mode tokens defined and tested in both modes?
- Mobile collapse explicit for high-variance layouts?
- Viewport stability: min-h-100dvh, never h-screen?
- Empty/loading/error states provided?
- Cards omitted in favor of spacing where possible?
- No AI Tells from Section 11?

If a single checkbox cannot be honestly ticked, the page is not done. Fix it before delivering.
